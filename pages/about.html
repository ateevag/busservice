About Us

Who we are!
Reserve ur Ride is the number 1 inter-city bus ticketing service in the world. Reserve ur Ride is a part of ibibo Group , India’s primary online travel group that also owns the leading hotels and air aggregator, goibibo.com

Our Credentials
Founded in 2006 in Bangalore, India, Reserve ur Ride now connects various cities across the globe with just a click of a button. Reserve ur Ride has revolutionized the online bus ticket booking industry by bringing together over 2,000 bus operators covering over 100,000 routes across countries.

So, Why Book With Us?
Reserve ur Ride provides bus travellers, the most uncomplicated and hassle-free booking experience ever.
Choose your destination, select bus based on user ratings, view the seat layout, choose convenient seats, and book your ticket in just a few clicks!
The Reserve ur Ride experience does not end with the booking.
In a series of industry leading features, Reserve ur Ride offers navigation to bus boarding points, real-time tracking of buses and information on rest stops.
Reserve ur Ride also partners with leading bus operators to provide exclusive ‘Red Deals” to customers booking on Reserve ur Ride. A dedicated 24*7 customer-care team is available to cater to all the passengers needs while they are on-the- go.
